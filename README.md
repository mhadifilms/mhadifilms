## 👋 Hey, I'm M Hadi

I'm a filmmaker & editor of 7+ years with a passion for exploring the boundaries of creativity through the latest technology. I believe in the power of prototyping with technology to unlock creative potential. Below, I've compiled some of my favorite eexperiments. Some are half-baked, and some never made it past a simple prototype. But the beauty is in the ability to have a prototype rather than just an idea lost in a notes app somewhere. Some of my favorite experiments include:


- [Arc Exporter](https://github.com/mhadifilms/arc-exporter): Switch to and from Arc Browser to Chrome, Zen, or Firefox... RIP Arc :(
- [DVR](https://github.com/mhadifilms/dvr): A DaVinci Resolve CLI & MCP Server for using the Resolve API to easily automate thousands of video editing tasks
- [TrailerDB](https://github.com/mhadifilms/trailerdb): The first database of film trailers across every language, movie, and TV series - all collected in one view with analytics and links.
- [Cropaway](https://github.com/mhadifilms/cropaway): A lightweight Mac desktop app for cropping and segmenting videos with simple video or bounding box export (supports AI tracking)
- [Save to Spotify](https://github.com/mhadifilms/SavetoSpotify): A Chrome extension that allows you to easily save music from any site to a Spotify playlist of choice


Feel free to play around, tweak, and experiment with any of the repos - have fun :)
