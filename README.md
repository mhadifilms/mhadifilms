## 👋 Hey, I'm M Hadi

I'm a multimedia creator of 7+ years with a passion for exploring the boundaries of creativity through the latest technology. I believe in the power of prototyping with technology to unlock creative potential. Below, I've compiled some of my favorite eexperiments. Some are half-baked, and some never made it past a simple prototype. But the beauty is in the ability to have a prototype rather than just an idea lost in a notes app somewhere. Some of my favorite experiments include:

- [Unrender](https://github.com/mhadifilms/unrender): Rendering an editor's timeline compresses everything into one file. Unrender breaks it back into shots and stems
- [DVR](https://github.com/mhadifilms/dvr): A DaVinci Resolve CLI & MCP Server for using the Resolve API to easily automate thousands of video editing tasks
- [TrailerDB](https://github.com/mhadifilms/trailerdb): The first database of film trailers across every language, movie, and TV series - all collected in one view with analytics and links
- [Cropaway](https://github.com/mhadifilms/cropaway): A lightweight Mac desktop app for cropping and segmenting videos with simple video or bounding box export (supports AI tracking)


Feel free to play around, tweak, and experiment with any of the repos - have fun :)
